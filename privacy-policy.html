This Privacy Policy describes how ASA STUDIO ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in the Invoice Pro mobile application ("the App").

Invoice Pro does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data to external servers. All data you enter in the app (client names, invoice details, amounts) is stored locally on your device only.

All invoice data, client information, and settings are stored exclusively on your device using local storage. We have no access to this data.

Invoice Pro does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

The app may request access to device storage solely to allow you to save and share PDF invoices. No other permissions are required.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.
